Palo Alto Municipal Code - Title 18 Zoning test

§ 18.12.040 R-2 Zone Regulations

(a) Permitted Uses: Multi-family residential dwellings are permitted in the R-2 zone.

(b) Density: The maximum residential density in the R-2 zone shall not exceed 15 dwelling units per acre.

(c) Height Limit: No building shall exceed 35 feet in height.

(d) Setbacks:
    - Front: 20 feet minimum
    - Side: 10 feet minimum  
    - Rear: 15 feet minimum

(e) Parking: Two parking spaces required per dwelling unit.

(f) Lot Coverage: Maximum lot coverage shall not exceed 40% of the total lot area.

(g) Open Space: Minimum of 200 square feet of usable open space per unit required.

Effective Date: January 1, 2020
Last Amended: March 15, 2023
