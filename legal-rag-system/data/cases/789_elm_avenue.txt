Project: 789 Elm Avenue Condominium Conversion  
Location: Palo Alto, California
Date: June 10, 2022

Applicant: Elm Properties Inc
Proposal: Convert existing 18-unit apartment building to condominiums

Project Details:
- Address: 789 Elm Avenue, Palo Alto, CA 94301
- Parcel Size: 1.1 acres
- Existing Units: 18 units (built 1985)
- Affordable Units: 2 units at moderate income (11% of total)
- No new construction - conversion only
- Existing parking: 36 spaces (2 per unit)

Zoning: R-2 (Multi-family Residential)
Existing Use: Legal non-conforming (18 units on 1.1 acres = 16.4 units/acre, exceeds current 15 units/acre limit)
No Density Bonus: Standard conversion process, not requesting additional density

Planning Commission Hearing:
- Date: June 10, 2022
- Vote: 7-0 APPROVED (unanimous)
- All commissioners in favor

Public Comments:
- 5 speakers total
- 5 in support (existing tenants pleased with conversion opportunity)
- 0 opposed

Conditions of Approval:
1. Provide 10% affordable units (2 units at moderate income) for 55 years
2. Architectural review for exterior improvements
3. Updated parking plan and striping
4. Tenant notification and relocation assistance per city ordinance
5. Right of first refusal for existing tenants
6. Updated as-built drawings required
7. Homeowners association documents subject to city review

Timeline:
- Pre-application meeting: March 2021
- Application submitted: June 2021
- Architectural review: September 2021
- Planning Commission hearing: June 2022
- Final approval: June 2022  
- Total time from application to approval: 12 months

Community Response: Highly supportive. Existing tenants appreciated opportunity for homeownership.

Staff Recommendation: Approval with conditions
Staff Report Highlights:
- Conversion allows tenants to become homeowners
- Building in good condition
- Provides affordable homeownership opportunities
- No increase in density

Final Outcome: APPROVED
Status: Conversion completed August 2022
Current Status: All 18 units sold as of December 2022
